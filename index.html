The Solution

A team of AI analysts.
A human making the call.

DealDesk AI deploys 5 specialized agents that coordinate in real time — each one a domain expert, each one leaving a live audit trail. No memo ships without a human decision.

⚡

Speed without sacrifice

From company name to full investment memo in under 5 minutes. Same rigor as a human team — fraction of the time.

🔍

Transparent by design

Every agent posts its reasoning to a live Band chat room. Any stakeholder can follow along, question, and verify in real time.

👤

Human always in the loop

The pipeline stops and waits for a human to read the full memo and make the final call. Approve, reject, or request changes.

How It Works

Three steps. One decision.

01

You name the target

Enter the company name and any context you have — sector, stage, recent news. That's it. DealDesk takes it from there.

02

5 agents go to work

Research, Risk, Valuation, and Writer agents run in sequence — each @mentioning the next through a live Band chat room you can watch in real time.

03

You make the call

A full investment memo lands in front of you. You read it. You decide: Approve, Reject, or Request Changes. No memo ships without your signature.

The Pipeline

5 agents. One mission.

@Research

Business Analyst

Digs into business model, market size, team, financials, and competitive landscape.

@Risk

Risk Officer

Identifies red flags and scores each risk: LOW, MEDIUM, or HIGH.

@Valuation

Financial Modeler

Builds a valuation range using revenue multiples and comparable transactions.

@Writer

Memo Author

Synthesizes everything into a structured, VC-grade investment memo.

@HumanReview

You

Read the memo. Make the call. The human is always the last node in the pipeline.

Powered by Band

Coordination through conversation

Most multi-agent systems are black boxes. DealDesk AI coordinates through Band — so the entire pipeline is visible as a conversation, not hidden in logs.

💬

Each agent has its own identity. They post under their own name, @mention the next agent, and build a traceable chain of reasoning.

🔗

No orchestration server needed. Agents pass context through the chat — Band is the message bus, the UI, and the audit log.

👁

Any stakeholder can follow along. Share the Band chat link and your deal team sees every agent's reasoning in real time.
